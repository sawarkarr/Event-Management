About Starlight Event Planner

From concept to celebration, Maharashtra's trusted event management company delivers magical experiences tailored to your unique vision.

Welcome to Starlight Event Planner, Maharashtra’s trusted name in wedding and event management. With a passion for creating unforgettable experiences, we specialize in planning elegant weddings, beautiful destination events, and memorable celebrations across the state.

From the smallest details to the grandest moments, our team ensures every event reflects your unique vision. We combine creativity, culture, and flawless execution to craft moments that last a lifetime.

Since our journey began, we've successfully planned numerous weddings, private parties, and corporate gatherings across Nagpur, Pune, Mumbai, and beyond. We take pride in offering personalized services tailored to each couple or client, ensuring every event feels truly special.

Our Vision: To become Maharashtra’s most loved and creative event management brand by designing stunning, joy-filled events rooted in tradition, yet elevated with modern flair.

Our Mission

  • Design visually stunning, joy-filled events
  • Deliver stress-free planning journeys
  • Celebrate stories with elegance and emotion
  • Bring destination wedding charm to your hometown

Why Choose Starlight?

  • Personalized and attentive planning
  • Clear, transparent process from start to finish
  • Expertise in local and destination weddings
  • Stylish concepts, premium décor & designs
  • Complete end-to-end event solutions

We proudly serve couples across Nagpur, Pune, Mumbai, Aurangabad — and we’re always excited to plan destination weddings wherever your heart desires.
